Student Enrollment Analytics

Comprehensive Data Analysis & Business Intelligence Project

Project Overview

This project analyzes student enrollment data from a multi-campus educational institution, providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making. The analysis covers 93,454 enrollment records across 5 campuses and 8 academic terms.

Key Business Insights

Revenue Optimization

Math and English subjects generate 40%+ of total revenue. Focus on expanding capacity for these high-demand subjects.

Campus Performance

Significant variance across campuses (20-30% difference). Top-performing campus strategies can be replicated.

Student Retention

60%+ of students enroll in multiple terms. Opportunity to improve retention for single-enrollment students.

Seasonal Patterns

Clear enrollment peaks during fall and spring terms. Resource planning should align with seasonal demand.

Technical Approach

  • Data Processing: Comprehensive data cleaning and validation pipeline
  • Student Identity Resolution: Advanced phone number-based deduplication
  • Feature Engineering: Time-series and demographic feature creation
  • Statistical Analysis: Cohort analysis, trend analysis, and comparative studies
  • Visualization: 12+ interactive charts and executive dashboards

Business Impact

  • Identified $15M+ potential revenue increase through improved retention
  • Developed strategic recommendations for campus optimization
  • Created actionable insights for subject portfolio management
  • Established KPIs for ongoing performance monitoring
